Jabalpur, MP, India. Having expertise in areas of Material science, Analog &
Digital Communication, Optoelectronic devices, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning have been resource person of Anveshika Vigyan Prasar Yojna initiated by IIT Kanpur. I have been holding key role in Atma Nirbhar Madhya pradesh, India and am a regular reviewer of various journals of international repute and has published many research paper in reputed journals.
